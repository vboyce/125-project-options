In this experiment, you will play a fun communication game with another Prolific worker. Before starting the game, you will be given further instructions and must pass a short quiz testing your understanding of the instructions. Please note that you may only do this experiment once so do not accept multiple versions of it!

We expect the average game to last approximately 15 minutes, including the time it takes to read instructions and wait for another player.

The actual duration of the game depends on the cooperation of both players. We strongly encourage you to only accept this task if you can start immediately, out of fairness to your partner.

If you find the game is running on the longer side, no need to rush or drop out -- leave a comment at the end and/or send us an email (vboyce@stanford.edu) and we will make sure you're compensated for your time! Please pay attention and do your best! Thank you!

Note: We recommend Chrome. We have not tested this experiment in other browsers.
